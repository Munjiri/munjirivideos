Our story begins in South Luangwa, Zambia—where the air hums with the deep “hu-hu-huh” of hippos, ilala palms dot the pink sky, and a monkey with a baby still clinging to her belly is trying to nick your chips.

Wildlife everywhere, amazing communities, and a deep connection to the land. We were lucky to live and work there, surrounded by all this beauty. But we couldn’t ignore what was happening in other places around us. 

Nature was declining, and species were gradually disappearing. And the people and organisations trying to protect it? Their stories weren’t being heard. We saw small businesses, charities, and brands doing incredible work, but they struggled to break through the noise. They had amazing missions but lacked the tools to tell their stories in a way that really resonated with people.

That’s why we started Munjiri Videos.
We wanted to take everything we know about video—storytelling, editing, lighting, camera work—and combine it with practical strategies to help purpose-driven brands share their work with the world. Whether it’s crafting a video or teaching you how to create great content on your own, we make the whole process simple, clear, and dare we say… fun?

Since then, we’ve worked with some amazing brands and charities and have seen the impact a good story can have. It gets people to care, to share and to act.

But we’re not stopping there. There are still so many stories out there waiting to be told, and we’d love to help you share yours.
