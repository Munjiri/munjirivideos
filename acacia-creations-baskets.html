ACACIA CREATIONS

Acacia Creation's talented artisans across the globe, use time-honored techniques passed through generations to bring one-of-a-kind pieces. Each and every product tells their story - a story of heritage, tradition, resilience, and creativity. Across the Southern Province of Zambia, Tonga women gather in small groups every day to weave baskets. It is believed that their unique weaving skills are inherited from the Ancestors who crafted baskets for gathering and serving food. Today, these baskets are still widely used in rural Zambian homes and skills are passed from mother to daughter.
