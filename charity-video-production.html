Charity Video Production, Turning Stories into Support

A Story-driven Charity Video Production Company

Charities do incredible things every day. But sometimes, it’s hard to explain just how much impact you’re making in a way that truly sticks with people. That’s where charity video production comes in. A well-made video can open eyes and motivate people to donate, volunteer, or spread the word.
At Munjiri Videos, we create honest, powerful videos that help your charity share stories that matter and build the support you need to keep going.

What Is Charity Video Production & Why It Matters

Charity video production is about using film to show the real difference your work makes. It’s more than just facts or stats, it’s about capturing moments and voices that bring your work to life.
People don’t just want to hear about causes they want to see and feel them. Video can show the people you help, the challenges they face, and the hope you’re giving. It’s one of the best ways to make your message clear and encourage action, whether that’s giving money, signing up to volunteer, or simply sharing your story.

Who We Work With As A Charity Video Production Team

We’ve worked with all kinds of charities, from small local groups to bigger national organisations. Our experience covers a wide range of causes including education, youth support, climate action, and community projects.
We understand the importance of telling stories ethically and respectfully. Every video we create is carefully made to to truthfully and thoughtfully portray the people and communities involved and to meet your needs without stretching your budget.

Our Approach to Charity Video Production

We believe storytelling in video should be natural and honest. No overly polished scripts, just real moments and genuine emotions.
Our team works patiently with you and your community, making sure everyone feels comfortable and heard. We know charity work isn’t always easy or straightforward, so we keep things flexible, simple, and stress-free.
We also understand budgets can be tight and deadlines short. So, we adapt and keep communication open throughout the whole process.

Our Charity Video Production Process 

Planning: We start with a chat to understand what you want to say, who you want to reach, and where the video will be used. Don’t worry if you’re not sure about everything yet, we’ll guide you through it.

Production: We handle all the filming with care and professionalism. Whether it’s on location or somewhere remote, we’ll organise everything and make sure your story is captured with respect.

Post-Production: This is where we put everything together, editing the footage into a flowing story, adding music or graphics, and refining the final video until it feels just right for you.

Charity Video Production FAQ's

What kinds of videos can you make?
We do fundraising videos, case studies, campaign films, training and onboarding videos, volunteer recruitment, anything that helps you share your message.
How much does it cost?
Every project is different, but we always aim to work within your budget. We offer transparent pricing and discounted rates for charities.
How long does it take?
Some videos take a week, others a few months, it depends on your timeline and the type of film. We’ll map out a schedule with you at the start.
Where do you work?
We’re based between Portugal and South Africa, but we work with clients across the world, from Kenya to Scotland, remotely or on the ground.
