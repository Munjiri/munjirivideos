A video production company that started by a hippo filled river

Hi! We’re Katy and Eugene and we met whilst living in a national park in Zambia. While filming our everyday and all the amazing wildlife for fun, we realized there were so many brands and organisations around us that had meaningful stories, but they just weren’t being heard.

So we created a thoughtful video production company that sits in the sweet spot between marketing strategy and documentary craft. Over the last 8 years, we’ve brought this approach to independent shops, impact brands, and names like CNBC and Michelle Obama.

With clear communication throughout and an organised process we handle all the research, planning, filming and editing as well as making you feel relaxed on camera. Building a visual world that your people can’t wait to step into and does the hard work for your business. Leaving you with more time to do the things you enjoy in your brand.

Brand

Social
