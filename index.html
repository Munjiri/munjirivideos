Hi, We're Katy and Eugene, two videographer's based in Portugal, working worldwide

Or you can think of us like your creative collaborators who handle all your video making stresses. Two filmmakers who met in a national park filled with hippos in Zambia, combining down to earth documentary-style with the look of a beautifully crafted film, building a world that your people can’t wait to step into.

While filming our everyday and all the amazing wildlife around us for fun, we decided to create a brand that helps people turn their best, most meaningful business ideas into beautiful, thriving realities.

Over the last 8 years, we’ve worked with grassroots organizations, independent shops, impact-driven brands, and a few larger names you might recognize, like CNBC and Michelle Obama. Now based between Portugal and South Africa, we combine a cinematic eye with practical marketing strategies to create videos that make people stop scrolling and want to work with you. And we help people who get completely frozen when the lens points at them, look like a natural!

Our Video Production & Creative Services

We bring your brand world to life through three distinct services, depending on where you are in your business journey.

Video Production
Complete, cinematic brand films and website hero videos. We handle the audience research, full production, and editing to build a beautiful visual world your people can’t wait to step into.


Social Media Video
For the busy founders who tackled their own content creation but are completely sick of the endless social media treadmill and want a library of high-quality, monthly video assets delivered straight to them so they can make people stop scrolling while saving time for the work they love.


1:1 Video Lessons
Tailored lessons for you or your team.  If you're tired of your just ok DIY videos, overthinking your marketing, or feeling awkward infront of the camera, and are ready to learn how to plan, film, and edit your own videos.
