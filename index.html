Our Story

A still morning, mist curls over the river, hippos grunt, a monkey makes a move on your breakfast. We’re Katy and Eugene and when we met in South Luangwa, Zambia we saw something strange: the people and brands doing the most meaningful work, protecting nature, building communities, preserving craft were often the least visible.

So we thought what if we used our camera skills and editing, to get these stories out there? But we quickly realised it wasn’t just about beautiful visuals; there was so much more to learn about marketing and storytelling, to truly reach the right people. 

Our first film was for a charity. Tiny budget. Huge heart. We poured everything into it and the right people saw it. Donations came in. Support grew. So we decided to go for it.

Since then, we’ve filmed stories all over, from Portugal to South Africa, UK to Kenya. We’ve shot in eco-lodges, filmed artisans at work, and helped camera-shy clients find their voice. And somehow… our work’s been shared with over 56.9 million people, including by Michelle Obama.

But growth brought challenges, pressure to become content machines, thinking too much about trends and algorithms.

So we took what we'd learnt, paused and looked back at those early videos the ones that felt real and came back to that. Because in a noisy world, it’s the quiet, meaningful stories that matter most. And we’re here to help tell them beautifully.
