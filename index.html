Hi, we’re Katy and Eugene, two filmmakers helping sustainable brands, creative brands, and social impact organizations share their stories through cinematic videos. We create video content for brands and organizations that champion creativity, community, and caring for nature.


Why Choose Munjiri Videos?

We know that creating video content can feel overwhelming, from technical equipment to figuring out how to tell your story in a way that connects with your audience. That’s why we make video production simple, down-to-earth, and collaborative, helping you bring your vision to life without the stress.

Whether you're an artisan brand preserving traditional crafts, a non-profit inspiring social change, or a sustainable business, we’re here to help you share your story with the world.

Our Approach to Storytelling

From ancient myths to modern movements, the stories we tell shape how we think, act, and see ourselves. We’re wired for stories, not just facts. They stick with us and guide our decisions. Even when we sleep, we dream in stories.

Our films highlight the small moments that make your brand special, leaving your audience with a sense that they’ve experienced a part of your journey. Whether it's a behind-the-scenes look at your craft process or a campaign video for your latest social impact project. Humans have always understood the world through stories.

Our Story


Our story begins in South Luangwa, where the air hums with the deep “hu-hu-huh” of hippos, ilala palms dot the pink sky, and a monkey with a baby still clinging to her belly is trying to nick your chips. Wildlife everywhere, amazing communities, and a deep connection to the land. We were lucky to live and work there, surrounded by all this beauty. But we couldn’t ignore what was happening in other places around us.

Nature was declining, and species were gradually disappearing. And the people and organisations trying to protect it? Their stories weren’t being heard. We saw small businesses, charities, and brands doing incredible work, but they struggled to break through the noise.

That’s why we started Munjiri Videos.We wanted to take everything we know about video, storytelling, editing, lighting, camera work and combine it with practical strategies to help purpose-driven brands share their work with the world. Whether it’s crafting a video or teaching you how to create great content on your own, we make the whole process simple, clear, and even fun!

Even Michelle Obama likes our work (she posted a video we shot to her 56.9 million followers on instagram.)
