FAQs About Learning Video Making

What kind of gear do I need to start creating videos?
You really don’t need fancy gear to get started. Whether you’ve got a phone, a basic camera, or some pro equipment, we’ll show you how to make the most of what you have. It’s more about the story than the setup.

Can I really learn video making if I’ve never done it before?
Totally. You don’t need any experience at all. We’ll walk you through everything step by step and keep it simple so you don’t feel overwhelmed. Everyone starts somewhere, and we’ve got your back.

How do I know if video is the right fit for my brand?
Video is an amazing way to connect with people, but it’s not the only option. We’ll help you figure out if it’s a good match for your message and goals and if it is, we’ll guide you on how to use it well.

What’s the best way to learn video making?
The best way to learn video making is by actually doing it with a little help. In our 1:1 sessions, we’ll focus on what you want to create and teach you hands-on, practical skills like filming, editing, and telling your story in a way that feels natural.

Do I need any background in video to join?
Nope! Whether you’re just getting started or you’ve tried a few things already, we’ll meet you where you’re at. We keep things relaxed, clear, and super practical so you can start learning video making without feeling lost.

How long does it take to learn video making?
It depends on what you want to do, but most people start feeling more confident after just a few sessions. We move at your pace and help you build skills step by step, with plenty of time to practice in between.
