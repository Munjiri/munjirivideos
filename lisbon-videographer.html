Lisbon Videographer: FAQ's

Q: How much input do I have in the video?
As much as you like! From the story and shot list to feedback during editing, you’re involved every step of the way. Your Lisbon videographer team handle the technical stuff and planning, but your ideas and vision guide the project.

Q: Do I need to be “good on camera”?
Not at all! We work with clients who’ve never been filmed before. As a Lisbon videographer we guide you step by step, making sure you feel relaxed and natural. Our goal is to capture your story authentically, so you don’t have to worry about awkwardness or forgetting what to say.

Q: What if I need changes after the video is finished?
We include a clear number of revisions in every project, and the process for extra edits is easy. We want you to be happy with the final product, and we’ll guide you through any tweaks without extra stress.
