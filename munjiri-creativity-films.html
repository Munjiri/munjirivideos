Creative Video Production & Brand Storytelling

Here are some of our creative video production and brand storytelling videos for sustainable brands, businesses, and artists. Whether you're a handmade jewelry brand, a slow fashion designer, or a homeware company championing craftsmanship, our videography brings your brand's vision to life.


Yewo: Sustainable Jewelry with Purpose

Yewo is more than just a jewelry brand, it’s also about ethical craftsmanship and social impact. Our video for Yewo captures their commitment to sustainability and community empowerment.
Sustainable Storytelling: Yewo uses recycled materials and 14k conflict-free gold to create their pieces.
Empowering Communities: The brand provides training and employment for artisans in rural Malawi.
Environmental Impact: For every product sold, Yewo plants a tree through local reforestation initiatives.
Behind-the-Scenes: Take a look at Yewo’s solar-powered workshop, where each piece is crafted with care and purpose.

Baabuk: Crafting Footwear with Wool

Baabuk creates comfortable, sustainable footwear with respect for both people and planet. Our video highlights their ethical production process and commitment to sustainable materials.
Sustainable Sourcing: Mulesing-free wool sourced from Serra da Estrela National Park, Portugal.
Craftsmanship with Tradition: Wool is processed at the historic Burel Factory, preserving traditional techniques and supporting local artisans.
Eco-Friendly Footwear: Wool’s renewable, recyclable qualities make Baabuk’s shoes both stylish and sustainable.
Behind-the-Scenes: A glimpse into Baabuk’s process where sustainability meets craftsmanship.

How a Video Can Transform Your Brand

A great video does more than just look good, it helps people connect with your brand in a real and meaningful way. Whether you're a small business or an established brand, here’s how the right video can make a difference:

Grab Attention & Keep It – People love watching videos! They’re more engaging than photos or text, meaning potential customers will stick around longer to learn about what you do.
Make Your Brand More Memorable – A well-made video helps you stand out and makes a lasting impression.
Show, Don’t Just Tell – Instead of just talking about your values, a video lets people see the craftsmanship, passion, and care behind your products.
Build Trust – Seeing the faces, hands, and real stories behind your brand makes it more personal and relatable.
Encourage Sales – People are far more likely to buy after watching a video that connects with them.


We make the whole process easy and enjoyable, working with you to create a video that truly represents your brand and resonates with your audience.
