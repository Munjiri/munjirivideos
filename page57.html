We’re Katy and Eugene!

Over the last 8 years, our cameras have taken us all over the place from Portugal and South Africa to the UK and Kenya. We’ve been lucky enough to film for small independent brands, local charities, and even global names like CNBC and Michelle Obama.

Our story actually started in a hippo filled national park in Zambia. We were both just filming the amazing nature and everyday moments entirely for fun when we met.

But while we were there, we noticed something that didn’t feel right, the people doing the most meaningful work, things like protecting wildlife, building up communities, or preserving beautiful crafts, were completely invisible online. They were just way too busy doing the actual, hands-on work to stop and film it.We wanted to change that.

So, we combined our production skills with a real understanding of marketing and storytelling to help. Today, we help thoughtful businesses and organisations stop overthinking their content and just share their world naturally, so they can get back to doing what they love.
