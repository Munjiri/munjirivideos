Videographer Porto Process

Research 
Wapping out the trusty notepad we research what you are selling, how it helps people and who it's for?

Planning 
We take all this research and put it into a structure (hook, problem, solution and result) as well as planning all the details for filming, logistics, locations, equipment, shot lists and interview questions.

Filming
Up early for filming, we carefully consider, framing, sound, lighting, colour whilst making you and your team feel as comfortable on camera as possible.

Editing
Coffee and computer time, editing begins, always keeping your audience and the story arc front of mind, we create curiosity, laying down the audio, adding in the beautiful and flowing visuals, mixing pacing, matching music and colour correction. We then collaborate with you to discuss any changes.

Final Delivery
Always amazing to see a finished video coming together from just a few scribbles in a notepad. We send you the finished version for your website, instagram, presentation, youtube or wherever you need it. We also guide you on how to share your video so you can get the most out of it.

Porto Video Production Beautiful Locations

Old Town & Ribeira
Cais da Ribeira: Colorful houses on the waterfront and views of the Eiffel-style bridge.
Rua de Bainharia: Steep, narrow historic alleyway with tiled facades and overhanging balconies.
Largo da Pena Ventosa: A tiny, hidden cobblestone square.

The Douro Waterfront & Landmarks
Jardins do Palácio de Cristal: Sweeping terraces overlooking the entire river valley.
Ponte de Dom Luís I (Top Deck): Walkway offering dramatic backdrops.
Miradouro da Vitória: A stone lookout over old terracotta rooftops and church towers.

Contemporary & Architectural
Casa da Música: Bold, minimalist concrete angles and giant glass walls.
Serralves Museum & Park: Ultra-clean, pink art-deco architecture.
São Bento Station: High-ceilinged atrium wrapped in blue-and-white hand-painted tiles.

Foz do Douro (The Coast)
Farol de Felgueiras: Historic stone lighthouse facing Atlantic waves.
Passadiços da Foz: Wooden boardwalks winding through coastal rocks,
