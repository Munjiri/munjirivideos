SABAHAR + CREATIVE WOMEN

Creative Women works closely with a group of talented artisans in Ethiopia called Sabahar. Sabahar is an Ethiopian Fair Trade company that produces handmade cotton and silk textiles. Celebrating the rich weaving tradition of Ethiopia. Sabahar support and train artisans to apply their ancient skills to modern and fresh designs. Creating the bridge between the artisan and the global market and providing a reliable income for families.
