Frequently asked questions about our travel video production

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes. We’re based in Portugal, but we’re often working in places like South Africa, the UK, and Kenya too. And if your project is elsewhere, we’re happy to travel to you.

Will filming disrupt our day-to-day or guests?

No, this is something we’re very careful about. We work with a small, low-impact setup and plan everything around your normal schedule, whether that’s guest check-ins, tours, or peak times. The goal is to blend in, not take over.
