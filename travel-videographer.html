Travel Videographer: Travel Video Production That Works

Hi! We're Katy and Eugene Two Travel Videographers


Honest Storytelling

You aren't looking for loud TikTok trends, aggressive speed ramps, and visuals that are disrespectful.

We don't rely on flashy editing tricks or treat local cultures like a backdrop for a trendy feed. Instead, we take the time to look closely, listen well, and capture the actual texture and local heritage of a place. The result is a quiet, beautiful film that tells an authentic story.

Clear Processes & Fixed Pricing

You really don't want amateur gear setups, slow emails, surprise fees and weeks of radio silence while editing on the road.

We treat the business side of filmmaking with absolute respect. You will get fast communication, clear timelines, and a single, transparent quote with zero hidden costs. This means our work stays on track and your film is delivered on time, no matter where we are.

Built for Every Screen

Sometimes shooters focus so much on capturing 9:16 vertical reels for social media that they fail to deliver high-quality widescreen footage for your website.

Your film should look beautiful on a widescreen desktop and a mobile phone. We plan and compose our shots carefully so they naturally fit both formats. 

Calm on Set (Making People Comfortable)

Intimidating, high-stress film sets with inflated egos that make local guides, hosts, or team members freeze in front of the camera.

The best moments happen when people forget the camera is there. We keep our presence on set small, quiet, and friendly. We don't bring big egos or chaotic energy. We know how to guide your team and local hosts so they feel entirely at ease, letting their natural warmth and genuine personality come through.

Making Sure Your Film is Found

Art for art's sake that looks pretty but never gets seen because it isn't optimized for search engines.

A beautiful film only works if people can actually find it. We balance our creative craft with practical search engine optimization knowledge. By handling backend details like YouTube metadata, keyword intent mapping, and clean video hosting parameters, we make sure your content is easily discoverable for travelers searching for their next destination.


Our Travel Video Production Services

We create video content that helps your business look better, feel clearer, and convert more customers, whether that’s on your website, YouTube, or social media.

Brand Video

This is your main story. It’s the kind of video people see on your website when they first discover you, the one that quickly explains who you are, what you do, and why it matters.

From one shoot we can also create supporting long-form content that helps people go deeper over time.
This can include: Website homepage videos, service or portfolio videos, educational or blog-style videos, YouTube content designed for search and long-term visibility.

Everything here is focused on: Clear storytelling, building trust, helping people understand your business quickly. Turning visitors into enquiries or customers.

Social Media Video

This is where your brand shows up every day. Short-form content designed for platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest, made to help people discover you, connect with you, and eventually buy from you.

We structure this into three simple types of content:

Growth Content (Top of Funnel): Helps new people find you, tips and how-to videos, educational content, broad, shareable ideas, entertaining or trending content.

Nurture Content (Middle of Funnel): Builds trust over time, behind-the-scenes moments, real-life, relatable content, stories from your work or process, day-to-day brand personality.

Sales Content (Bottom of Funnel): Helps people decide, clear explanations of what you offer, product or service breakdowns testimonials and client results, simple calls to action.

Everything we create is designed to work together: Long-form content builds your foundation (your website + YouTube) Short-form content keeps you visible and growing every day. One tells your story in depth. The other keeps your story alive in people’s feeds.

Our Travel Videographer Process

Research
Wapping out the trusty notepad we research what you are selling, how it helps people and who it's for?

Planning
We take all this research and put it into a structure (hook, problem, solution and result) as well as planning all the details for filming, logistics, locations, equipment, shot lists and interview questions.

Filming
Up early for filming, we carefully consider, framing, sound, lighting, colour whilst making you and your team feel as comfortable on camera as possible.

Editing
Coffee and computer time, editing begins, always keeping your audience and the story arc front of mind, we create curiosity, laying down the audio, adding in the beautiful and flowing visuals, mixing pacing, matching music and colour correction. We then collaborate with you to discuss any changes.

Final Delivery
Always amazing to see a finished video coming together from just a few scribbles in a notepad. We send you the finished version for your website, instagram, presentation, youtube or wherever you need it. We also guide you on how to share your video so you can get the most out of it.
