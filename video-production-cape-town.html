We know video production in Cape Town can feel intimidating, from concept to final edit, so we take care of all the technical details, allowing you to focus on what matters: sharing your story.

We take care of everything in-house, including:

Finding the best spots around Cape Town
Directing and filming
Editing, color grading, and sound
Making versions for social media – like Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts

Cape Town Video Production - Featured Work

Coral & Hive
We partnered with Coral & Hive to create a brand film that mirrors the rhythm and reverence of their work. Shot between their Cape Town studio and expansive looms, the story follows each handmade rug, from ethically sourced fibre to final woven piece.

Soul Design Jewellery
Shot in Kalk Bay and surrounding neighbourhoods, this video for Soul Design captured the beauty of everyday adornment. Handmade jewellery, African craftsmanship, and a team that gives back this is the kind of video production Cape Town creatives come to us for.

Hands of Honour
We documented the life and legacy of Paul Talliard, founder of Hands of Honour, a Cape Town-based social enterprise turning waste into opportunity.

Dehye Collective
With Dehye Collective, we created a vibrant visual showcase of their Ghanaian-inspired interiors and accessories. The content celebrated heritage, colour, and the tactile power of culture.
