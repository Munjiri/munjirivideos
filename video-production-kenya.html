Our Video Production Kenya Work

At Munjiri Videos, we offer video production Kenya for organisations and brands that care.

There’s a lot of noise out there. With big-budget ads and viral trends, it’s easy for meaningful work to get lost. But if your organisation is driven by purpose, building communities, protecting nature, or creating with care, you deserve to be seen.
You’re doing the hard work. We’re here to make sure your story is heard.

Whether you’re a non-profit in Nairobi, an eco-lodge in the Maasai Mara, or an artisan brand rooted in tradition, your story matters. And we’re here to tell it beautifully.

Video Production Kenya Team

We’re Katy and Eugene, a filmmaking team offering purpose-led video production across Kenya and beyond.
We met in South Luangwa, Zambia, and quickly saw a pattern, the people and organisations doing the most meaningful work were often the least visible. That didn’t feel right to us. So, we combined our love for creating beautiful visuals with storytelling and marketing and started making videos for them.

Since then, we’ve filmed in Kenya, Malawi, South Africa, Portugal, the UK, and more. We’ve worked with nonprofits, ethical homeware brands, nature organisations, and local makers. One of our videos was even shared by Michelle Obama with her 56.9 million followers.

Our Kenya Video Production Process

We believe the best videos come from genuine collaboration and a relaxed, thoughtful process. Here’s how our video production in Kenya works:
Discovery Call – We start by listening. What’s your story? Who do you want to reach?
Story Planning – We develop a video concept that’s emotionally engaging and aligned with your goals.
Production – We keep things calm, creative, and collaborative.
Editing & Crafting – We shape a clear, real story that highlights what makes your work unique.
Delivery & Strategy – You’ll receive a ready-to-share film, plus tips on how to get the most from it.
