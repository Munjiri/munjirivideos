Our Video Production Process

I used to think the video production process was all about your camera and filming, but I soon realised that’s just one part and if you don’t get those other parts in place, it’s unlikely you’ll have a great video. So here are the steps we follow to make sure every video is set up for success.

Wapping out the trusty notepad, we research what you are selling, how it helps people and who it's for?

Then planning, we take all this research and put it into a structure (hook, problem, solution and result) as well as planning all the details for filming, logistics, locations, equipment, shot lists and interview questions.

Up early for a day of filming, we carefully consider, framing, sound, lighting, colour whilst making you and your team feel as comfortable on camera as possible. 

Coffee and computer time, editing begins, always keeping your audience and the story arc front of mind, we create curiosity, laying down the audio, adding in the beautiful and flowing visuals, mixing pacing, matching music and colour correction. We then collaborate with you to discuss any changes.

Always amazing to see a finished video coming together from just a few scribbles in a notepad. We send you the finished version for your website, instagram, presentation, youtube or wherever you need it. We also guide you on how to share your video so you can get the most out of it.

We create story filled videos that connect with the right people. Our documentary meets cinematic style tells your story in a down to earth human way, creating videos that keep working for you long after they're published.

Our Video Production Process: Getting to Know You and Your Goals

Notepad in hand, we focus on understanding your message, audience, and goals.
This part is can often be overlooked, but it's really important to get it in place.
We’ve seen many organisations invest in video without clearly defining what they want viewers to remember. Even high-quality footage struggles when the message isn’t clear.

Product: What Are You Selling?
One of the most common mistakes is trying to say too much in one video.
A strong video has one clear objective a service, a message, or a transformation. Everything else can come later. Whether it’s attracting clients, donors, or awareness, we define the single message that matters most.

Benefits: How Does It Help People?
People don’t really connect with products or services, they connect with what changes for them.
So we focus on what your work actually does for people:

What problem does it solve?
What changes because of it?
Why should anyone care?

For example, the product might be a piece of jewellery, but it's really about the story behind it, handmade, ethically made pieces that feel personal and unique. When that's clear, people are much more likely to pay attention and connect with it.

Audience: Who Is This For?
A video can’t speak to everyone, so it’s important to know who you’re talking to from the start. We identify your audience early on and shape the message and story around what matters most to them. Whether it's a social impact project that needs to connect with donors, communities, and stakeholders, or a business video aimed at potential customers.

Finding the Human Story
Once people understand what you do, they want to understand who you are. The best moments often come when people stop performing and as cliche and annoying as it sounds are themselves.

Our Video Production Process: Planning

This is where ideas become a practical video production plan, helping everything run smoothly on filming day / days and making the most of everyone’s time.

We often use a simple storytelling structure that guides viewers from interest to action:

Hook → Problem → Solution → Result
From there, we shape the video production around:
A strong opening that grabs attention
A relatable challenge or story
Clear benefits
Real proof through examples or testimonials
A simple next step for the viewer

As well as the structure our video production plan includes logisitics, locations, equipment, shot lists and interview questions.

Our Video Production Process: Filming

Filming is about much more than cameras and lighting. While we're constantly thinking about framing, sound, colour, lighting, and all the technical details behind the scenes, our real focus is creating an environment where people feel comfortable being themselves.

By the time the filming day comes around, everything is planned and prepared, allowing the process to feel relaxed and natural. Rather than worrying about what to say or how to act, you can focus on sharing your story and connecting with the people who matter most.

The most effective videos aren't about perfect performances, they're about making people care. When people speak from experience and focus on helping the viewer, their message feels more relatable and that's when people remember it.

Our Video Production Process: Editing

Editing is where the story really comes together. With your audience and the story arc always front of mind, we shape the raw footage into a clear, engaging narrative through pacing, structure, and emotion.
From selecting the strongest interview moments and weaving in beautiful visuals, to sound design, music, colour grading, and creating moments of curiosity, every edit is made with intention. For social media content especially, attention is everything, every second needs to earn its place.

Once we've crafted the first version, we work closely with you, through a simple review process, refining the details until the story feels just right.
