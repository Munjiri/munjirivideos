 Our Video Production Process

Product - Find out exactly what you're selling and how it helps people.

Audience - Research who we are making this video for and what problems they're facing.

Stories - We listen to your stories, the highs, the lows, the little details.

Structure - Give your video a clear shape, create interview questions and shot lists.

Film and Audio - Filming time! Throughout the video production process we carefully consider colour, light, framing and sound that build the world your audience can step into.

Edit - Pick the very best moments, follow the story arc, cut, cut, cut, and add music and audio.

Send - You’ll get your finished video or videos, plus info on how and where to use them.
